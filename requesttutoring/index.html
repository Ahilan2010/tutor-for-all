Tutor-For-All provides one-on-one math and reading tutoring for K–5 students from low-income families in Houston — at absolutely no cost to you. Fill out the form below and we'll handle the rest.

Who This Program Is For

Our tutoring is reserved for K–5 students from low-income households in the Greater Houston area. We ask a few short eligibility questions in the form below so we can direct our limited resources to the families who need them most. All information you provide is kept confidential and used only for eligibility screening.

Your Child's Safety & Privacy Come First

  • All tutors are vetted high school students who meet strict academic requirements.
  • All sessions are conducted online via video call (Zoom, Google Meet, or similar).
  • A parent or guardian is welcome — and encouraged — to watch any session from the same room.
  • Your family's personal information is never shared with third parties.

What Happens After You Submit

1

We Review Your Request

Our team reviews your submission and verifies eligibility within 3–5 school days. We may reach out with a brief follow-up question.

2

We Match Your Child with a Tutor

We pair your child with a tutor based on their grade level, the subjects needed, and your scheduling preferences.

3

We Contact You to Schedule

We'll reach out via your preferred contact method (email or phone) to confirm the match and set up the first session at a time that works for you.

4

Sessions Begin — Free

Your child's first session is set. All sessions are free, structured, and focused on math and reading goals. Progress is shared with you throughout.

If you're unsure whether your child qualifies or have any questions before filling out the form, don't hesitate to reach out directly.
