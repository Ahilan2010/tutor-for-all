How to Support Us

Two ways to give — every contribution goes directly to helping low-income students.

💵

Monetary Donation

Cash donations help us cover program operations, printing, outreach materials, and future educational resources for students and families. Every dollar makes a direct difference for a child who needs it.

Donate via PayPal

Secure payment processed through PayPal. You do not need a PayPal account to donate by card.

📚

Books & School Supplies

Donate educational books, workbooks, pencils, notebooks, and other school supplies. We distribute donated materials directly to low-income students in Houston — often attached to information about our free tutoring program.

Donate Resources

Fill out the form and we'll arrange a drop-off or pickup for your donation.

Outreach & Program Operations

A Student-Led, Community-Funded Program

Tutor-For-All has no paid staff and no administrative overhead. Every tutor is a volunteer. Every dollar you donate goes to work immediately — buying books, printing outreach materials, and supporting the tools that make learning possible for students who have very little.
